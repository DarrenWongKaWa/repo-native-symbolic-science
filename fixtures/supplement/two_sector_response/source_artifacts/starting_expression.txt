R_ij = ∫₀^Λ dk/k [A_ij(k) + B_ij(k)] + O(ε²)

where:
  A_ij(k) = f(k) δ_ij + g(k) k_i k_j / k²
  B_ij(k) = h(k) (δ_ij - k_i k_j / k²)

This is the starting expression for the two-sector response kernel.
The integral runs over the radial momentum coordinate k from 0 to the UV cutoff Λ.
The measure is dk/k consistent with logarithmic scaling in d = 2 + ε dimensions.
The O(ε²) indicates that this expression is exact to linear order in ε.
